# Become an Affiliate

If you create tutorials, videos, blog posts, or social media content, you can make money by helping more people discover Serial Studio. The affiliate program is designed for content creators, educators, publishers, and community builders who want to recommend the app and earn commission on successful referrals.

#### Why Promote Serial Studio?

Affiliate partnerships are one of the simplest ways to monetize useful content. You send customers our way, and we pay you when those referrals turn into sales.

#### How Do I Make Money?

Share your affiliate link on your website, blog, YouTube channel, newsletter, forum posts, or social media accounts. Lemon Squeezy tracks clicks and completed transactions so commissions can be attributed to your account automatically.

#### How Much Can I Earn?

You can earn up to **$159.80** in commission for each referral that results in a successful sale. There is no cap on how much you can earn by promoting Serial Studio.

#### Payout Minimum

Affiliate payouts require a minimum balance of **$10.00** before they are processed. This helps reduce fraud and administrative overhead in the affiliate program.

#### When Do Payouts Happen?

Payouts are handled on **NET30** terms to account for refunds and chargebacks. For example, commissions generated in January are paid on **March 15**.

#### How Do I Sign Up?

The Serial Studio affiliate program is hosted by the Lemon Squeezy affiliate hub. Create a Lemon Squeezy account, apply to the program, and start sharing your affiliate link once approved.

Become an affiliate: <https://store.serial-studio.com/affiliates>
